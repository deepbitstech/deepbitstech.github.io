Uncover the Hidden Threats in Software Supply Chain

A software product often contains packages, libraries, or modules made by third parties, and these third-party components may again contain components from other sources. This is known as software supply chain. As a result, there may be vulnerabilities or malware that are packaged into a software product from unknown sources. We provide next-generation vulnerability and malware identification technique to secure software supply chain.

Innovate the Analytics for Securer Cyberspace

The analytics engine, backed by our top-notch research on cybersecurity analytics techniques funded by NSF, DARPA, ONR, AFRL, etc., integrates the state-of-art static & dynamic analysis techniques, and deep learning techniques.

Uncover Every Piece of Code

With the innovative binary analysis techniques, our disassembling engine can uncover every piece of malicious code, no matter how it is obfuscated. This helps us identify the most stealthy malware.

Learn Intelligence from Binary Code

Empowered by deep learning technology, our analytics engine learns intelligence directly from binary code. The intelligence helps us fight against cyber attacks by identifying malware, malware authorship, vulnerabilities in computer systems, etc.
