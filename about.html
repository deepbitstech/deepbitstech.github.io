About Us Innovating for Securer Cyberspace

Backed by over ten years’ top-notch research on cybersecurity analytics techniques funded by NSF, DARPA, ONR, AFRL, etc., DeepBits Technology is constantly innovating the analytics for securer cyberspace. DeepBits provides fundamental security analytics tools (binary analysis engine, classification engine, etc.) used to build cyber security solutions. We call this “Security-Tool-As-A-Service”. Our solution reduces the R&D expenditure of our customers and lowers entry bar of the increasing cybersecurity market. With the lowered entry bar, we anticipate that more innovations will be put into practice. As a result, with the increased competition and reduced R&D expenditure, we expect to reduce the cybersecurity spending of the companies and government.
